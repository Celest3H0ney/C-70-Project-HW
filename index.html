Campus Life

Front Of School

Has been provuiding education since 1830

Francis Howell Middle has been providing education since 1830. It has been named National School Of Character twice in the past decade. The school is a lively place with a variety of classes and good teachers. Every year the school welcomes 6th graders with Transition Day, which is what helps the newcomers cope with the new school. The school is an accepting and welcoming place.

Winner Of FHMS Track Race

Winning the race is a good thing, as the Francis Howell Middle track is large-sized. The winners get recognition the following days, when the staff announce the winners' names on the school intercom.
